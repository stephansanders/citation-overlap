Citation-Overlap

Citation-Overlap is a tool to identify similar or duplicate citations from multiple bibliographic database searches. It imports lists from MEDLINE, Embase, and other databases, integrates them into a common format, and measures citation similarity to output a combined list showing duplicates with similarity scores. This tool facilitates deduplication for systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and other comprehensive literature searches.

Learn more on our GitHub page.

Enter the URL of a Google Sheets to edit:

  1. Add blank sheets for each database.

    2. By default, we add sheets for MEDLINE, Embase, and Scopus.

  2. Import your citation lists

    3. Go to the "File" menu and select "Import". Select your first citation file saved from the databases. Choose the options to "Replace current sheet" and leave the rest of the options with their defaults. Repeat for the rest of your citation lists.

  3. Find overlapping citations

    4. Performs deduplication, which finds similarities among citations and groups potential duplicates. This step may take a few minutes to complete. As lists are filtered, they will be inserted as new sheets, followed by the "overlaps" sheet when deduplication is complete.

  4. (Optional) Auto-fit column widths of the new sheets

    5. Resizes columns to fit text up to a max width. This step may take several minutes to complete.

  5. (Optional) Reset by removing new sheets

    6. Removes the "clean" and "overlaps" sheets to allow rerunning the deduplication.
